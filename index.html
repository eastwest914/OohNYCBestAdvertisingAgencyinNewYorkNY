Out-Of-Home Advertising

Out-Of-Home advertising is one of the most powerful ways to build brand awareness in a city like New York. Ooh NYC places your message across transit shelters, kiosks, subway stations, and high-traffic corridors throughout Manhattan and NYC. Our OOH campaigns deliver consistent impressions that reinforce your brand identity wherever your audience goes.

Billboard Advertising

Billboards remain one of the most iconic and effective forms of city advertising. Ooh NYC offers premium billboard advertising placements across Manhattan and the greater NYC area, from digital displays in Times Square-adjacent zones to classic large-format boards along major avenues and highways. Our billboard inventory puts your brand in front of millions of eyes every single day.

Street-Level Advertising

Street-level advertising connects your brand with pedestrians at eye level, creating intimate and memorable encounters. Ooh NYC specializes in placing ads on bus shelters, newsstands, benches, and sidewalk kiosks in some of Manhattan's most walked blocks. We identify the highest foot-traffic zones in your target neighborhood and secure placements that put your brand directly in front of potential customers.

Mobile Billboards

Mobile billboards are the most dynamic form of outdoor advertising available in NYC. Ooh NYC deploys a fleet of large-format mobile billboard vehicles that drive customized routes through your target neighborhoods, event zones, and commercial corridors. Unlike fixed placements, mobile billboards follow your audience, delivering your message at the times and places that matter most.

Urban Advertising

Urban advertising is about owning the visual landscape of the city. Ooh NYC creates comprehensive urban advertising strategies that combine multiple placement types across Manhattan and the five boroughs for saturation-level brand visibility. From Midtown to Brooklyn, our urban campaigns are designed to create a sense of omnipresence for your brand within the city environment.

Public Space Advertising

New York City's public spaces are some of the most valuable advertising real estate in the world. Ooh NYC secures placements in parks, plazas, transit hubs, and cultural destinations across the five boroughs. Public space advertising reaches people during moments of leisure and discovery, making them more receptive to brand messaging. Our team handles all permitting, logistics, and installation so your campaign goes live smoothly and on schedule.
